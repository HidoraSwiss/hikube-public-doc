# Introduction to Hikube

## **What is Hikube?**

**Hikube** is an enterprise private cloud platform offering modern infrastructure with maximum sovereignty and security guarantees.

### **Infrastructure and Availability**

**Sovereign Cloud with Data Hosted in Switzerland**
- All your data remains strictly on Swiss territory, ensuring compliance with local regulations and providing maximum peace of mind regarding data protection.

**Very High Availability with Replication across 3 Datacenters**
- Ensure your applications remain accessible at all times thanks to our redundant infrastructure, distributed across three geographically distinct datacenters in Switzerland.

**100% Green IT Datacenter**
- Commit to the environment with our datacenter powered by renewable energy, minimizing the carbon footprint of your IT operations.

### **Compute Services**

**Kubernetes as a Service**
- Deploy and manage your containerized applications easily with our Kubernetes service, which offers automated orchestration, scalability, and simplified management.

**Linux and Windows VM Instances**
- Host your applications on Linux or Windows instances, tailored to your specific needs and optimized for maximum performance.

**GPU as a Service**
- Harness the power of high-performance computing with our GPU as a Service offering, ideal for demanding workloads such as artificial intelligence, machine learning, and complex simulations.

### **Security and Isolation**

**Data Encryption**
- All stored data is encrypted when needed, ensuring that your sensitive information is protected even when at rest.

### **Automation and Integration**

**Controllable via API and Terraform**
- Automate and manage your cloud infrastructure efficiently thanks to our API and Terraform support, facilitating integration and management of your resources.

**Enterprise-Oriented and Quality**
- Our offering is specifically designed to meet enterprise requirements, providing robust, reliable, and high-quality solutions for your professional needs.
